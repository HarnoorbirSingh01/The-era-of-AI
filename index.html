The Era of AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that involves building machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI systems can perceive their environment, interpret data, and reason to decide the best actions to achieve a goal.

Weak artificial intelligence (AI) is a type of AI that is limited to a specific area. It is also known as narrow AI.While weak AI systems can perform certain tasks, they are not as capable as strong AI systems.

Artificial general intelligence (AGI) is the representation of generalized human cognitive abilities in software.The intention of an AGI system is to perform any task that a human being is capable of.

Super AI, or Artificial Superintelligence (ASI), is a hypothetical form of AI that is not yet possible to achieve. ASI is a type of AI that surpasses human intelligence and can perform any task better than a human.
